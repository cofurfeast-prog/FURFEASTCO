# FurFeast Co. - Product Overview

## Project Purpose
FurFeast Co. is a full-featured Django-based e-commerce platform specialized in pet food and accessories. The platform provides a comprehensive shopping experience with real-time communication features, enabling pet owners to purchase quality products while receiving instant support and order updates.

## Value Proposition
- **Specialized Pet Commerce**: Dedicated platform for dog food, cat food, and pet accessories
- **Real-time Engagement**: WebSocket-powered chat and notifications for immediate customer support
- **Community Building**: FurFeast Family feature connects pet owners
- **Complete Order Management**: End-to-end order tracking from placement to delivery
- **Business Intelligence**: Comprehensive admin dashboard with analytics and insights

## Key Features

### Customer-Facing Features
- **Product Catalog**: Browse categorized products (dog food, cat food, accessories)
- **Shopping Experience**: Cart management, wishlist, product reviews and ratings
- **User Authentication**: Registration, email verification, password reset, profile management
- **Order Tracking**: Real-time order status updates with tracking numbers
- **Live Chat Support**: WebSocket-based real-time messaging with admin support
- **Notification System**: Instant notifications for order updates and messages
- **Community**: Join FurFeast Family with pet details and profiles
- **Content**: Blog articles about pet care and nutrition

### Admin Features
- **Analytics Dashboard**: Sales metrics, revenue tracking, customer insights
- **Product Management**: Full CRUD operations for inventory
- **Order Processing**: Status updates, tracking number management
- **Customer Support**: Centralized chat interface for all customer conversations
- **Marketing Tools**: Flash sales, promo codes, email campaigns
- **Content Management**: Blog post creation and hero image management
- **Automated Notifications**: System-triggered order status notifications

## Target Users

### Primary Users (Customers)
- Pet owners seeking quality food and accessories
- Users who value real-time support and order transparency
- Community-oriented pet enthusiasts

### Secondary Users (Administrators)
- E-commerce managers handling product inventory
- Customer support staff managing live chat
- Marketing teams running campaigns and promotions
- Business analysts tracking sales and customer behavior

## Use Cases

### Customer Journey
1. Browse products by category or search
2. Add items to cart or wishlist
3. Complete checkout with PayPal integration
4. Receive real-time order status notifications
5. Track order progress with courier tracking
6. Use live chat for support inquiries
7. Leave product reviews after delivery
8. Join FurFeast Family community

### Admin Operations
1. Monitor sales and revenue through dashboard analytics
2. Manage product inventory and pricing
3. Process orders and update shipping status
4. Respond to customer chat messages in real-time
5. Create flash sales and promotional campaigns
6. Send targeted email campaigns to customers
7. Publish blog content for customer engagement
8. Analyze customer behavior and purchase patterns

## Technical Highlights
- **Real-time Architecture**: Django Channels with WebSocket support
- **Cloud Storage**: Supabase integration for scalable file storage
- **Payment Processing**: PayPal gateway integration
- **Modern UI**: TailwindCSS for responsive design
- **Production Ready**: Docker containerization with Defang deployment
